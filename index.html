    About me

    I am an Oxford University graduate and scholar emeritus, based in Tokyo and working as a business analyst and communication consultant. I support British companies entering the Japanese market and coach Japanese professionals in high-level Business English for international contexts. With experience spanning consultancy, research, and corporate environments, I bring a practical approach to help companies get the best out of their teams when using English in meetings, negotiations and written communication. My work sits at the intersection of international business, cross-cultural communication, and professional standards expected in global organisations.
